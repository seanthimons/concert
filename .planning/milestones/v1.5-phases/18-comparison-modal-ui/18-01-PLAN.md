<objective>
Replace the resolution dropdown for disagree rows with a Compare button that opens a side-by-side candidate comparison modal. Users see all candidates with enriched metadata (CASRN, formula, MW) in card layout, select one, confirm, and the disagreement is resolved. Pinned disagree rows get a "Change" link to reopen the modal. En masse priority resolution remains unchanged.

Purpose: Give users a richer, more informed comparison experience for resolving chemical disagreements instead of a terse dropdown.
Output: Modified mod_review_results.R with Compare button, modal UI, and resolution handlers.
</objective>
